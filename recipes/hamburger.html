Hamburger

Description

A hamburger is a classic American dish that brings together the savory flavors of a juicy beef patty, nestled between two soft buns. Topped with fresh lettuce, ripe tomatoes, crispy pickles, and a drizzle of ketchup and mustard, itâ€™s a satisfying meal that can be customized to suit any palate. Whether grilled on a barbecue or cooked on a stovetop, the hamburger is a versatile option perfect for picnics, barbecues, or a cozy dinner at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 blend)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Optional: cheese slices, bacon, or avocado

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into four equal patties, pressing a slight indentation in the center of each.
  3. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired doneness.
  5. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  6. Toast the buns on the grill or in a skillet for about 1-2 minutes.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing the patty on the bottom bun, then adding lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, and mustard.
  8. Top with the other half of the bun and serve immediately.
