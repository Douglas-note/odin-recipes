Pizza

Description

Pepperoni pizza is a beloved classic that never fails to satisfy. With its crispy, golden crust and generous layers of spicy, flavorful pepperoni, this pizza is the ultimate comfort food. The combination of the gooey melted mozzarella cheese and the zesty sauce creates a delightful balance that pairs perfectly with the savory toppings. Whether you’re enjoying a quiet night at home or celebrating with friends, this pizza is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Making pepperoni pizza at home allows you to customize it just the way you like it. From the thickness of the crust to the amount of cheese and toppings, you can adjust everything to suit your preferences. This recipe includes a simple homemade dough, which adds a personal touch that elevates the dish. Get ready to experience the joy of creating your own delicious pepperoni pizza!

Ingredients

For the Dough:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
For the topping:
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 4 oz pepperoni slices
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano(optional)
  • Olive oil for drizzling

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a large bowl, mix the flour, yeast, and salt together. Gradually add the warm water and olive oil, stirring until a rough dough forms. Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place it in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm spot for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  2. Preheat the Oven: After the dough has risen, preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
  3. Shape the Pizza: Punch down the risen dough and transfer it to a floured surface. Roll or stretch the dough into a 12-inch round. Transfer it to a pizza peel or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Add Toppings: Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese generously over the sauce, then layer the pepperoni slices on top. If you like, sprinkle dried oregano for added flavor.
  5. Bake the Pizza: Carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or place the baking sheet in the oven. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Finish and Serve: Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute. Slice it up and enjoy the deliciousness of homemade pepperoni pizza, perfect for sharing or enjoying all to yourself!
