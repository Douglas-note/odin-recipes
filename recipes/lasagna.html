Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish that layers wide pasta sheets with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. This hearty casserole is baked to perfection, resulting in a bubbly, golden top that contrasts beautifully with the tender, flavorful layers beneath. Ideal for family gatherings or special occasions, lasagna is a comforting meal that embodies the essence of home cooking, bringing warmth and joy to any table.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef or sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat and stir in the marinara sauce. Simmer for a few minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and a pinch of salt and pepper until smooth.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and béchamel (if using). Repeat the layers, finishing with noodles topped with meat sauce and remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired and serve warm.
